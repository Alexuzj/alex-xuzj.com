Activating Urban Spaces: Revitalizing Sunnyside Yards

Transforming Community Ideas into Reality

Collaborators: Vicky Luo (Cornell M.S.AUD '24)

In a city as vibrant as New York, the potential for community-driven urban spaces is immense. Our project focuses on transforming Sunnyside Yards into a dynamic space where community ideas can flourish without bureaucratic hurdles.

Our concept reimagines Sunnyside Yards by integrating flexible urban spaces that empower residents to realize their ideas, from book swaps to dance parties. Inspired by historical insights and the Garden City movement, we aim to create an inclusive environment that encourages interaction and participation.

The design incorporates existing and new community spaces with informal areas for engagement. Key elements include a transformed sunken railway into a landscaped strip and multifunctional pop-up spaces, fostering connectivity and vibrant community life. Our toolkit connects community needs with potential sponsors, ensuring sustainable development.
