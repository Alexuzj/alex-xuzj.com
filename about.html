About Me :)

When I was curled up in my bed in a southern Chinese city, watching Woody Allen's Manhattan, I never imagined that I would one day find myself in New York, deeply intertwined with this city's vibrant fabric.

In China, I studied Urban and Rural Planning at Beijing Normal University Zhuhai. In the beautiful coastal city of Zhuhai, I embarked on my design journey and began pondering the intricate relationship between planning, architecture, and the urban landscape.

In 2023, I ventured to Cornell's AAPNYC to pursue a Master of Science in Advanced Urban Design (M.S.AUD). Under the guidance of many brilliant scholars and peers, I started focusing on stakeholder engagement, community coordination, and how urban economics and politics shape design strategies that impact people's lives.
