Hi, I'm Alex. I'm an urban designer, design strategist
and photographer.

My curiosity drives me to dissect and understand urban challenges, transforming them into innovative solutions. This inquisitive spirit fuels my journey in urban design, where each project enhances my approach and ensures success.

A key focus of my work is urban strategy development. I am intrigued by the interplay of design and system strategies, and how they foster sustainable, equitable communities. Understanding people and their needs is central to my approach, enabling me to create compelling narratives and visuals that drive impactful urban development.
